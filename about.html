Our story

Built to level the
playing field for SMEs.

Lanna Mountain exists because great small businesses lose to weaker competitors every day — not because they're worse, but because they're invisible online and their phones go unanswered.

The founder
"I kept seeing talented business owners lose to competitors with bigger marketing budgets — not because they were worse, but because they were invisible."

I spent years working across digital marketing, watching the same pattern repeat: a skilled tradesperson, a dedicated clinic owner, an excellent restaurateur — all losing customers to bigger operations simply because their online presence was weak and their phone kept ringing out.

The technology to fix this existed. AI voice agents, automated SEO, local search optimisation. But it was expensive, complex, and built for enterprise companies with dedicated marketing teams.

So I built Lanna Mountain to change that. To take the same AI-driven systems that enterprise companies pay tens of thousands for each month, and make them accessible, affordable, and fully managed for SMEs across Australia, New Zealand, ASEAN, and the UK.

We're a small, focused agency. We don't take on every client — we take on clients we know we can genuinely help. Every service we offer is something I've deployed myself and proven to work.
