FAQ

Common questions about
AI voice agents.

Modern AI voice technology sounds remarkably natural. We use the latest voice synthesis to create agents that sound warm and professional. Many customers don't realise they're speaking to an AI — and in our experience, most don't mind when they do, as long as it's helpful and fast.

The agent is configured with clear escalation rules. For any call that falls outside its scope, it takes a message and promises a callback, or transfers the call to your team in real-time if you prefer. Nothing gets dropped.

We handle all the technical setup. The agent can take over your existing number, operate as an overflow when you're busy, or be set up as a dedicated after-hours number — whatever works best for your workflow. No new hardware required.

We support Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, Acuity, Cliniko, and most major booking platforms. For CRMs we integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, ActiveCampaign, and more. If you use something not on this list, let us know — we'll check compatibility.

We currently support English, Thai, Vietnamese, Bahasa Indonesia, Malay, Tagalog, and Mandarin. Additional languages can be added — contact us with your requirements and we'll advise on feasibility.

Pricing depends on call volume and features required. We offer a free strategy call where we'll scope your exact needs and provide a transparent quote. There are no hidden fees and no lock-in contracts. Most SME clients start from $297/month.
