How Much Does Marketing Cost in Thailand? Honest 2026 Guide

By Tom, Founder of Lanna Mountain — Published 11 June 2026

Pricing for marketing services in Thailand varies enormously — from a few thousand baht per month from a freelancer to 100,000+ from a large Bangkok agency. Here's what you actually get at different price points, and how to decide what's right for your business.

One of the most common questions we get from Thai SME owners is: "What should I budget for marketing?" The honest answer is that it depends entirely on what you're trying to achieve, which channels you're investing in, and who you're hiring to do the work. This guide gives you real pricing for the services that actually move the needle for Thai SMEs, without the vague ranges and meaningless "it depends" answers you usually get.

Local SEO (Google Maps and Local Rankings)

Local SEO in Thailand — optimising your Google Business Profile and building the review velocity and citation consistency needed to rank in local search packs — typically costs:

  • Basic GBP setup and optimisation: 5,000–15,000 THB one-time
  • Ongoing local SEO management: 8,000–25,000 THB per month, covering review strategy, GBP post management, local citation building, and monthly reporting
  • Local SEO + website content: 15,000–40,000 THB per month, adding service pages, blog content, and on-page SEO

What to expect from a legitimate local SEO provider: specific deliverables each month (not vague activity reports), measurable outcomes (GBP impressions, direction requests, calls tracked), and realistic timelines (3–6 months to meaningful improvement in competitive markets).

Content SEO and Agentic SEO

Content-driven SEO — building the articles and guides that rank for research-phase searches — is what separates high-performing websites from those that are simply "present" online. Pricing in Thailand:

  • Traditional content SEO: 20,000–60,000 THB per month for a meaningful content programme (4–8 articles per month, properly optimised)
  • Agentic SEO: Comparable or lower monthly cost, but with 5–10x the output — 20–40+ articles per month — because AI systems produce content at a fraction of the per-piece cost of human writers, while maintaining quality through editorial oversight

Our agentic SEO service is specifically designed to deliver the comprehensive content coverage that builds topical authority at a cost structure that makes sense for Thai SMEs rather than requiring enterprise budgets.

Google Ads

Google Ads in Thailand is generally more affordable than Western markets on a cost-per-click basis, but varies significantly by industry. For healthcare, legal, and financial services, CPCs can reach 100–300 THB. For hospitality, tourism, and retail, CPCs are typically 20–80 THB.

A minimum realistic Google Ads budget for Thai SMEs: 15,000–25,000 THB per month in ad spend, plus 8,000–20,000 THB per month in management fees if using an agency. Below these thresholds, there's insufficient data to optimise effectively and the cost per acquisition is typically uneconomic.

AI Voice Agents

AI voice agents for Thai businesses are priced based on call volume and capability. For a small Thai business handling 100–300 calls per month, expect 8,000–20,000 THB per month for a fully configured AI receptionist system. The ROI typically comes from a combination of reduced missed call rate, staff time saved, and 24/7 coverage that captures after-hours leads.
