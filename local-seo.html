Local SEO is a specific, technical discipline. Getting it right means showing up in the Google Map Pack, being trusted by the algorithm, and being visible at every local touchpoint where customers search.

📍

Google Business Profile Optimisation

Your GBP is your most important local SEO asset. We fully optimise every field — categories, services, photos, posts, Q&A — and keep it active with weekly updates that signal relevance to Google.

🗂️

Citation Building & NAP Consistency

We build and audit your business listings across directories, maps, and industry platforms. Consistent Name, Address, and Phone details across every listing is the bedrock of local authority.

⭐

Review Strategy & Management

Reviews are a top local ranking factor. We implement a systematic review generation strategy — prompting happy customers at the right moment — and help you respond to all reviews, positive and negative.

📝

Local Content Creation

Suburb and city-level landing pages, local blog content, and neighbourhood guides — all optimised for the exact searches your customers are making. Content that ranks locally and converts.

🏗️

Schema Markup & Structured Data

We implement LocalBusiness, Service, and Review schema on your website. This helps Google understand your location, operating hours, and services — directly improving Map Pack eligibility.

📊

Local Rank Tracking & Reporting

Track your Map Pack position and local organic rankings for every target suburb and keyword. Monthly reports show where you've moved and what's driving the changes — in plain English.

The process

From invisible to
top of the Map Pack.

Local SEO has a clear foundation — build it right, maintain it consistently, and the Map Pack follows. Here's exactly how we do it.

01

Local presence audit

We audit your current Google Business Profile, check citation consistency, review your existing rankings in the Map Pack, and benchmark you against your top local competitors.

02

GBP & on-site foundation

We fully optimise your Google Business Profile, fix NAP inconsistencies across all major directories, and add schema markup to your website. This is the foundation everything else is built on.

03

Review generation system

We set up a simple, automated review request sequence that prompts your recent customers at the right time. More reviews, higher ratings, stronger local authority — without you having to ask awkwardly.

04

Local content & citations

We build local landing pages, write suburb-targeted content, and continue growing your citation profile. Each piece of content and each citation strengthens your local authority signal.

05

Ongoing management & reporting

We keep your GBP active with weekly posts, monitor and respond to reviews, continue building citations, and report monthly on ranking movements. Local SEO is maintained, not set-and-forget.
